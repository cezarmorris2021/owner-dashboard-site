Cold Call Script

Opener:
“Hi, this is Cezar with Sentinel Zero. Quick question before I say why I’m calling — do your employees use their phones for any part of the business?”
Problem frame:
“That’s exactly why I’m calling. Most businesses don’t realize their biggest security risk is actually employee phones — bad texts, weak logins, risky apps, and lost devices.”
Discovery:
“If one of your employees clicked a bad link in a text today, what would happen?”

“If a phone was lost right now, can you lock or wipe it immediately?”

“Do you have any real control over what apps are being used for work?”
Transition:
“That’s what we fix without turning your business upside down.”
Close:
“Let’s put a quick mobile security review on the calendar. What’s better, later today or tomorrow?”

Objection Handling

“We already have IT.”
“Perfect. Most IT support handles computers and networks. Mobile is usually the blind spot. That’s where we come in.”
“We’re too small.”
“Smaller teams usually feel one mistake harder. One bad login or phone issue can hit a small company fast.”
“Send me something.”
“Absolutely. Before I do, what matters more to you — preventing phishing texts, controlling lost devices, or tightening employee phone access?”
“Not interested.”
“No problem. Is that because it’s already handled well, or because it’s just not a priority right now?”
